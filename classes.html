Classes & Schedule

At Buckley School of Dance, we offer a wide variety of classes to suit dancers of all ages and skill levels. Our instructors tailor each session to help you build technique, confidence, and a lifelong love of dance.

Ballet

Our ballet classes focus on classical technique, posture, and musicality. From basic barre work to advanced choreography, students develop grace, strength, and discipline.

  • Ideal for ages 7 and up
  • Builds core strength, flexibility, and coordination
  • Prepares dancers for performances and competitions

Jazz

Jazz dance combines energetic movements with expressive style. Our jazz classes emphasize rhythm, flexibility, and sharp technique, encouraging students to explore creativity.

  • Open to ages 8 and older
  • Improves agility and performance skills
  • Includes classic and contemporary jazz styles

Hip Hop

Hip Hop classes focus on street dance styles and modern choreography, promoting self-expression and confidence through dynamic movement and rhythm.

  • All ages welcome
  • Emphasizes freestyle and group choreography
  • Encourages personal style and creativity

Tap

Tap dance classes teach rhythm and timing with percussive footwork, combining classic techniques with modern beats.

  • Recommended for ages 7+
  • Enhances coordination and musicality
  • Great for building confidence in timing and expression

Contemporary

Contemporary dance classes emphasize fluidity, storytelling, and emotional expression through movement. Students develop strength, flexibility, and improvisation skills.

  • Best suited for ages 10 and up
  • Focus on technique and creativity
  • Preparation for performance and choreography

Pre-Dance (Ages 3-5)

Our pre-dance program introduces toddlers to basic movement, rhythm, and creative play to build a foundation of dance skills in a fun and supportive environment.

  • Fun musical games and movement
  • Develops motor skills and coordination
  • Encourages social skills and creativity
