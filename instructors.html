Meet Our Instructors

Katie Buckley

Founder & Lead Instructor

Katie brings over 25 years of professional dance experience and a passion for teaching. She specializes in ballet, jazz, and contemporary dance, blending classical technique with creative expression.

Katie has trained internationally and has led numerous award-winning student performances. Her teaching philosophy focuses on nurturing confidence and joy in every dancer.

Jessica Lee

Jazz & Hip Hop Specialist

Jessica is known for her dynamic energy and innovative choreography. She has a background in competitive dance and street styles, bringing a fresh, fun approach to every class.

She encourages students to develop their own style while mastering technique and rhythm.

Michael Chen

Tap & Contemporary Instructor

Michael’s expertise lies in rhythm and storytelling through dance. He combines tap’s percussive beats with contemporary’s fluidity to create engaging performances.

He is passionate about helping students discover their unique voice and improve their technical skills.
