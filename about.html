About Buckley School of Dance

Our Founder: Katie Buckley

Katie Buckley is a seasoned dance teacher and studio owner with over 5 years of professional experience in various dance styles including ballet, jazz, hip hop, and contemporary. Her passion for dance began at a young age and led her to train intensively at renowned schools across Canada and internationally.

In 2023, Katie founded the Buckley School of Dance with the vision of creating a welcoming and nurturing environment where dancers of all ages and skill levels could thrive. Her approach combines disciplined technique with creativity and self-expression, inspiring students to grow both as dancers and individuals.

Beyond teaching, Katie is committed to community involvement and regularly organizes workshops, charity performances, and outreach programs to share the joy of dance with wider audiences.

Our Mission & Values

  • Excellence: We provide high-quality dance training grounded in technique and artistry.
  • Inclusivity: Our doors are open to all—regardless of age, background, or experience.
  • Creativity: We encourage innovation and personal expression in every dancer.
  • Community: We foster a supportive family atmosphere inside and outside the studio.
  • Growth: We focus on personal and technical development with performance opportunities at every level.

Achievements & Community Impact

Since opening, Buckley School of Dance has grown into a respected institution known for producing competition winners, scholarship recipients, and confident performers. Our students have been featured in local media and invited to perform at prominent Toronto cultural events.

We actively participate in charity galas and community festivals, believing that dance is a powerful tool to inspire and unite people. Katie and the entire team are proud to contribute to Toronto’s vibrant arts scene.
